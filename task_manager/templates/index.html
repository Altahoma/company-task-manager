Welcome to the Company Task Manager Django Project!

This powerful web application simplifies task management within your company or organization, making it easier than ever to collaborate and stay organized. Here's a brief guide on how to use it:

1. Registration and Login:

  • If you're a new user, start by registering an account. Click on the "Register" link or button on the homepage.
  • Fill in the required information, such as your username, email, and password.
  • Once registered, you can log in securely using your credentials by clicking on the "Login" link or button.

2. Task Creation:

  • After logging in, you can create new tasks by clicking on the "Create Task" button on tasks page.
  • Provide details for the task, including the title, description, due date, and assignees.
  • Click "Submit" to add it to the system.

3. Task Status Tracking:

  • Keep track of task statuses to monitor progress.

4. Task Searching:

  • Easily find specific tasks by using the search feature.
  • Enter the task title or keywords in the search bar to locate tasks quickly.

5. User Management (Admins Only):

  • Administrators have additional privileges to manage user accounts and roles.
  • Access the admin panel (superuser credentials required) to control user accounts and task types.
